The SMCH Sports Society

Our Purpose

The SMCH Sports Society is the official body responsible for organizing and promoting all sports and athletic activities within Southern Medical College & Hospital. We believe physical well-being is crucial for medical students' success and overall health.

Core Values

  • **Teamwork:** Encouraging collaboration and mutual support across batches.
  • **Discipline:** Upholding fair play and respect for rules and opponents.
  • **Excellence:** Striving for the highest standards in performance and organization.
