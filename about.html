Our Mission & Vision

Fostering a culture of health, discipline, and competitive excellence.

The SMCH Sports Society

Our Foundation

The SMCH Sports Society was founded with the core objective of integrating physical activity and competitive sports into the rigorous academic life of medical students. We believe that a balanced approach, encompassing mental and physical well-being, is crucial for developing future healthcare professionals.

Our Core Values

  • Teamwork: Building synergy, respect, and communication skills essential for both the field and the operating room.
  • Leadership: Providing opportunities for students to manage, organize, and captain teams and events.
  • Discipline: Instilling the importance of commitment, adherence to rules, and dedication to personal fitness goals.
  • Sportsmanship: Promoting fair play and mutual respect among competitors, win or lose.

Get Involved

We welcome all students, regardless of skill level, to join our events. Whether you are looking for serious competition or just a way to stay active, the SMCH Sports Society has a place for you. Follow our official Facebook page for practice timings and upcoming event registrations.
